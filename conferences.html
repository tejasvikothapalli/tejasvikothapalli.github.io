Conferences

  • Leadership and Competitive Excellence Conference (LACE)

    Date TBA

    A non-competitive conference, LACE hosts multiple speaker events and workshops to allow DECA members to improve their business skills, both in the real world and in the DECA sphere.

  • Silicon Valley Career Development Conference (SVCDC)

    Date TBA

    This is it: the first competitive conference of the year. Students put their past few months of effort to the test and compete against everyone else in the district. Performance at this conference does NOT affect eligibility to attend the State Conference.

  • State Career Development Conference (SCDC)

    Date TBA

    With the judge's feedback from the last conference, students fix their mistakes and head to another round of competitions, this time against the entire state of California. Winners here will then head to the International Conference.

  • International Career Development Conference (ICDC)

    Date TBA

    Those who make it here battle it out against the best competitors of each state and country to determine the world champions. But at the same time, they get to have their fair share of fun adventures and memorable times with the rest of the chapter.
