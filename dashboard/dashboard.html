SCDC Sign Up

January 14, 2017

Our second competitive conference of the year is fast approaching! The State Career Development Conference (SCDC) will be held in Santa Clara from March 2-5, 2017. After a successful showing at SVCDC, weâ€™re excited for another opportunity for Lynbrook DECA to compete!

Anyone interested in attending must have all of the indicated items and forms completed and turned in by Friday January 20, 2017. The conference has moved up registration deadlines this year, so as a result we will not be able to accept late form under any circumstances. The cost of the conference this year is $290 for registration and hotel.

Written competitors, please note that there has been a NEW CHANGE regarding written submissions. This year, SCDC will be implementing an online turn-in system so we will no longer be collecting printed plans. The written plan turn-in deadline will be February 14, 2017 at 11:59 PM. Email your written plans as a pdf file (under 4 MB) to presidents@lynbrookdeca.org. You will not need to turn in your presentation or presentation materials, only the plan itself. Also note that this is a hard deadline imposed by the conference and not by Lynbrook DECA, so a result, if you miss this deadline there will be little we can do. Please be timely about this turn-in!

To-do list for interested attendees:

  1. Fill out online SCDC registration form.
  2. Update Event Selection on the Lynbrook DECA website.
  3. Bring signed, hard copies of the following forms to any officer:
    • Printout of your transcript indicating your current or previous enrollment in a CTE course (NOT NEEDED if you have attended SVCDC this year)
    • FUHSD Trip Permit (NOT NEEDED if you have attended SVCDC this year)
    • FUHSD Release Permit (You will need to get signatures from ALL your teachers since you will be missing all day Friday, however you will have to communicate to your 6th and 7th period teachers that you will be absent for their class on Thursday as well.)
    • Form D
    • Form E
  4. Bring $290 check addressed to Lynbrook ASB (with your name and "Lynbrook DECA" in the memo line) to any officer. Please reach out to our adviser Mr. Fulk at brad_fulk@fuhsd.org if you will have difficulty affording this fee, but would still like to attend.
  5. Inform your parents of the upcoming SVCDC Parent Meeting and Informational Night on Wednesday, January 25th. The exact time of this meeting is tentative, but will likely take place at around 6PM.

Sign Up for SVCDC

November 10, 2016

Our first competitive conference of the year is coming up! The Silicon Valley Career Development Conference (SVCDC) will be held in San Jose from January 6-8th, 2017. This is the opportunity for members to compete and win awards in the roleplay and written events we have been preparing for in the past few months!

Anyone interested in attending must have all of the indicated items and forms completed and turned in by Monday, November 13th. Due to the hecticness of registration, late forms will NOT be accepted. The cost of the conference this year is $210 for registration and hotel. Please reach out to our adviser Mr. Fulk if you will have any difficulty affording this fee but would still like to attend.

As DECA is instituting the CTE requirement across all schools, members will be required to provide proof of having been in, or being currently enrolled in a CTE class. You can find and print out a copy of your class transcripts via Infinite Campus.

This year, along with registration we will be requiring all interested attendees to write a short message to Santa! This is a part of Lynbrook DECA's participation in the Macy's Believe campaign, which donates $1 to the Make-a-Wish foundation for every letter sent. Completed letters will be turned in with the physical forms.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact vpoperations@lynbrookdeca.org.

To-do list for interested attendees:


  1. Fill out google form
  2. Fill out event selection on profile
  3. Bring signed hard copies of the DECA Medical Form , Form D, Form E, and the FUHSD Release Permit to any officer (you need your 6th and 7th period teacher signatures for permission to skip class on Jan. 6th on Release Permit Form)
  4. Bring $210 check addressed to Lynbrook ASB (with your name and "Lynbrook DECA" in the memo line) to any officer
  5. Inform their parents of the upcoming SVCDC Parent Meeting and Informational Night on Wednesday, November 16th in the Library.
  6. Bring adorable letter to Santa to any officer
  7. Bring latest Progress Report to prove CTE course registration.

Sign Up for LACE

October 9, 2016

The first conference of the year, LACE, is finally here! It takes place during November 11-13, and is a purely NON-COMPETITIVE CONFERENCE. This means that the conference is for DECA members to learn about the club, attend valuable workshops, listen to world-renowned guest speakers, network with other DECA members, and get valuable tips about their own competitive events for future conferences. If you are interested in attending, please sign up on this form. The cost of the conference is $180 (make check payable to lynbrook ASB), and the deadline to sign up and turn in all forms is October 13th. If you have any questions, feel free to contact vpoperations@lynbrookdeca.org.
